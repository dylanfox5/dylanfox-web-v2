about-me

Hey there! My name is Dylan; I have a passion for solving problems. I was first introduced to the development world in 2015 when I took my first programming course and learned Python. From then on, I floated between several programming languages and niches -- finally landing on front-end & analytics development.

I've had the privilege to receive my start in the development industry with Evolytics, a top-class analytics consulting firm and one of the fastest growing in the market. As an Analytics Developer, my main focus is working with clients to ensure they are collecting the data they desire in the most efficient way.

Outside of work, you can find me spending time with family & friends, enjoying all things soccer-related, on the disc golf course, or pursuing a side-project that I'm curious about.

Here are some technologies I have experience with:

  • JavaScript
  • HTML & CSS (Bootstrap)
  • Python
  • R
  • Java
  • Adobe Experience Cloud
